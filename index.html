Introduction to HTML

What is HTML and its role?

HTML is a markup language that serves as the structure of a webpage.

Why use Semantic HTML Elements vs Divs?

Semantic HTML elements make the markup more readable, accessible, and
specific. It makes it easier to work with.

Why learn HTML?

There are many reasons to learn HTML, including opening up job prospects,
for fun, or simply to keep up with technology and better understand the digital
world.
